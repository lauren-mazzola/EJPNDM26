Packing Recommendations

  • 🧥 Pack layers! Lows can dip to ~40°F, highs can reach ~80°F — you'll likely experience both in the same trip
  • ☔ Bring a travel umbrella or light rain jacket — NC spring showers happen with little warning
  • 👟 Wear comfortable walking shoes — the conference venue is half indoor, half outdoor. You'll be on your feet all day!
  • 💊 Pack allergy medicine! Pollen counts in Raleigh can be very high in March — tree pollen especially. NC is known for its pollen season starting in early spring
  • 🕶️ Sunglasses — sunny days can be bright, especially outdoors
  • 💧 Reusable water bottle — hydration stations throughout the venue
  • 🔋 Portable phone charger / power bank — long days on your feet with the event app open
🤧

Allergy Season Alert

March is peak allergy season in Raleigh! Oak, birch, and cedar pollen can be extremely high. If you're sensitive to tree pollen, we strongly recommend packing your preferred antihistamine (Claritin, Zyrtec, Allegra, etc.) and eye drops. Taking an antihistamine the day before you arrive can help!
